# ACCEPTABLE USE POLICY

**Last Updated: February 9, 2026**

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") outlines the rules and standards for using the **Dayo** mobile application. By using the App, you agree to follow these rules.

Violations may result in account suspension, termination, or legal action.

---

## ALLOWED USES

You may use Dayo to:

✅ Create and manage your player profile  
✅ Join or create teams and clubs  
✅ Find and book basketball courts  
✅ Schedule matches and invite friends  
✅ Request referees for games  
✅ Participate in leagues and tournaments  
✅ Track your stats and rankings  
✅ Post photos and updates  
✅ Connect with other players and teams  
✅ Share court reviews and feedback

---

## PROHIBITED USES

You may **NOT** use Dayo to:

### 1. **Illegal Activities**

❌ Violate Philippine laws or regulations  
❌ Promote gambling or illegal betting  
❌ Facilitate drug use or illegal substances  
❌ Engage in human trafficking or exploitation  
❌ Sell counterfeit or illegal goods

### 2. **Fraud and Deception**

❌ Create fake or multiple accounts  
❌ Impersonate another person, team, or organization  
❌ Falsify match results or rankings  
❌ Manipulate ELO ratings or statistics  
❌ Use bots, scripts, or automation tools  
❌ Reverse engineer or hack the App

### 3. **Harmful Content**

❌ Post hate speech, threats, or violent content  
❌ Share sexually explicit or pornographic material  
❌ Upload content depicting minors inappropriately  
❌ Promote self-harm or suicide  
❌ Spread false information or fake news  
❌ Post defamatory or slanderous content

### 4. **Harassment and Abuse**

❌ Bully, harass, or intimidate other users  
❌ Send unwanted messages or spam  
❌ Stalk or doxx other users  
❌ Discriminate based on race, religion, gender, or disability  
❌ Threaten physical violence

### 5. **Intellectual Property Violations**

❌ Upload copyrighted photos/videos without permission  
❌ Use trademarks or logos illegally  
❌ Steal content from other users  
❌ Plagiarize team names or branding

### 6. **Disruptive Behavior**

❌ Spam posts, comments, or notifications  
❌ Create fake bookings or cancel repeatedly  
❌ No-show for matches without notice  
❌ Disrupt leagues or tournaments  
❌ Interfere with court operations

### 7. **Data Scraping and Misuse**

❌ Scrape or download user data without permission  
❌ Use automated tools to collect information  
❌ Sell or share user data with third parties  
❌ Create databases of user profiles

### 8. **Payment Fraud**

❌ Refuse to pay court fees after booking  
❌ Dispute legitimate charges with referees  
❌ Use stolen payment methods  
❌ Scam users with fake payment confirmations

---

## CONTENT STANDARDS

All content you post must:

✅ Be **respectful** and **appropriate** for all ages  
✅ Be **accurate** and **truthful** (no misinformation)  
✅ Comply with **Philippine laws** and community standards  
✅ Respect **intellectual property rights**  
✅ Be **relevant** to sports, basketball, or the Dayo community

If you see a violation of this policy, please report it:

📱 **In-App Reporting**:

- Tap the "⋯" menu on posts/profiles

📧 **Email**: support@dayo.ph

---

## CONSEQUENCES OF VIOLATIONS

Depending on severity, we may:

### **First Offense** (Minor Violations)

- Warning notification
- Content removal
- Temporary feature restriction (7 days)

### **Second Offense** (Repeated Violations)

- 30-day account suspension
- Removal of all content
- Loss of verification badge

### **Third Offense / Severe Violations**

- **Permanent account termination**
- IP ban
- Report to authorities (if illegal)
- Legal action (if applicable)

### **Immediate Bans** (Zero Tolerance)

These violations result in **instant permanent ban**:

- Child exploitation
- Threats of violence
- Doxxing or stalking
- Large-scale fraud or scamming
- Hacking or security breaches

---

## APPEALS

If you believe your account was suspended or banned in error:

1. Email **appeals@dayo.ph** within **14 days**
2. Include:
   - Your account email
   - Reason for suspension/ban
   - Explanation of why it was in error
3. We will review and respond within **7 business days**

**Note:** Decisions are final after the appeal process.

## COURT OWNER RESPONSIBILITIES

If you manage a court, you **must**:

- Accept or decline bookings in a timely manner
- Keep your court information accurate (address, rates, hours)
- Maintain safe and playable conditions
- Honor confirmed bookings
- Resolve disputes professionally

**Violations:**

- Fake court listings → Permanent ban
- Repeated no-shows or cancellations → Account suspension
- Safety violations → Immediate removal from platform

---

## REFEREE RESPONSIBILITIES

If you are a referee, you **must**:

- Arrive on time for assigned matches
- Officiate fairly and professionally
- Maintain valid certifications (if required)
- Set accurate rates and availability
- Communicate clearly with teams

**Violations:**

- Bias or unfair officiating → Rating penalties
- Repeated no-shows → Account suspension
- Fake certifications → Permanent ban

---

## LEAGUE ORGANIZER RESPONSIBILITIES

If you organize leagues, you **must**:

- Set clear rules and schedules
- Manage registration fees transparently
- Officiate disputes fairly
- Update standings and results promptly
- Ensure participant safety

**Violations:**

- Mismanagement of fees → Permanent ban
- Rigged results → Account termination
- Safety negligence → Legal liability and ban
