# COMMUNITY GUIDELINES

**Last Updated: February 9, 2026**

Welcome to the **Dayo** community! 🏀

Dayo is built on respect, sportsmanship, and love for the game. These Community Guidelines help ensure everyone has a positive, safe, and enjoyable experience.

---

## OUR VALUES

🏀 **Sportsmanship First** – Win or lose, we play with honor and respect  
🤝 **Respect Everyone** – Treat others the way you want to be treated  
⚡ **Stay Positive** – Encourage, don't discourage  
🛡️ **Safety Matters** – Keep the community safe for all users  
📸 **Share the Love** – Celebrate great moments and inspire others

---

## RESPECT AND KINDNESS

### DO:

✅ Be kind and supportive  
✅ Congratulate opponents on good plays  
✅ Welcome new players and help them learn  
✅ Use respectful language  
✅ Give constructive feedback  
✅ Celebrate diversity

### DON'T:

❌ Insult, mock, or belittle others  
❌ Use offensive language or slurs  
❌ Make threats or intimidating comments  
❌ Discriminate based on race, religion, gender, or ability  
❌ Harass or bully anyone  
❌ Troll or provoke arguments

---

## FAIR PLAY AND INTEGRITY

### DO:

✅ Play by the rules  
✅ Report match results honestly  
✅ Respect referee decisions  
✅ Show up on time for matches and bookings  
✅ Communicate clearly with your team  
✅ Accept wins and losses gracefully

### DON'T:

❌ Cheat or manipulate rankings  
❌ Create fake accounts to boost stats  
❌ Falsify match scores  
❌ Sabotage other teams  
❌ Use performance-enhancing substances  
❌ Bribe referees or officials

---

## CONTENT AND POSTING

### DO:

✅ Share basketball highlights, game photos, and updates  
✅ Post motivational content  
✅ Share court reviews and recommendations  
✅ Celebrate team achievements  
✅ Use appropriate captions  
✅ Credit photographers and content creators

### DON'T:

❌ Post offensive or inappropriate images/videos  
❌ Share graphic violence or injuries  
❌ Upload sexually explicit content  
❌ Post copyrighted material without permission  
❌ Spam irrelevant content  
❌ Share misinformation or fake news  
❌ Post content depicting minors inappropriately

---

## COMMUNICATION

### DO:

✅ Respond to messages and invitations promptly  
✅ Communicate cancellations or absences as soon as possible  
✅ Use clear and friendly language  
✅ Provide helpful feedback  
✅ Confirm bookings and match times

### DON'T:

❌ Ghost or ignore important messages  
❌ Send spam or unsolicited messages  
❌ Use profanity in public comments  
❌ Harass users with repeated messages  
❌ Share personal contact information publicly

---

## SAFETY AND HEALTH

### DO:

✅ Play within your physical limits  
✅ Warm up before games  
✅ Use proper safety gear (if applicable)  
✅ Report unsafe court conditions  
✅ Stop play if someone is injured  
✅ Respect COVID-19 or health protocols (if in effect)

### DON'T:

❌ Play while injured or impaired  
❌ Engage in reckless or dangerous play  
❌ Ignore safety warnings  
❌ Encourage unsafe behavior  
❌ Attend games if you're sick

---

## TEAMS AND CLUBS

### DO:

✅ Be a good teammate  
✅ Attend practices and games  
✅ Support your captain's decisions  
✅ Contribute to team discussions  
✅ Represent your club positively

### DON'T:

❌ Start drama or conflicts within the team  
❌ Poach members from other clubs  
❌ Create duplicate or fake teams  
❌ Abandon your team mid-season  
❌ Disrespect your captain or coach

---

## BOOKINGS AND PAYMENTS

### DO:

✅ Honor confirmed bookings  
✅ Arrive on time for court reservations  
✅ Pay court fees and referee rates promptly  
✅ Communicate payment issues directly with the provider  
✅ Leave courts clean after use

### DON'T:

❌ Make fake bookings  
❌ No-show without notice  
❌ Refuse to pay agreed fees  
❌ Damage court property  
❌ Dispute legitimate charges

---

## LEAGUES AND TOURNAMENTS

### DO:

✅ Read and follow league rules  
✅ Register your team on time  
✅ Attend all scheduled matches  
✅ Respect league officials and organizers  
✅ Report disputes through proper channels

### DON'T:

❌ Register multiple times or under fake names  
❌ Forfeit matches without notification  
❌ Argue with officials or organizers  
❌ Attempt to rig or fix matches  
❌ Disrupt league operations

---

## REFEREES

### For Players Using Referees:

✅ Treat referees with respect  
✅ Accept their decisions gracefully  
✅ Pay agreed fees promptly  
✅ Provide honest ratings after matches

❌ Don't argue calls excessively  
❌ Don't insult or threaten referees  
❌ Don't refuse to pay after the game

### For Referees:

✅ Officiate fairly and impartially  
✅ Arrive on time  
✅ Communicate clearly with both teams  
✅ Maintain certifications

❌ Don't show bias or favoritism  
❌ Don't accept bribes  
❌ Don't officiate if unqualified

---

## RESPECT FOR COURT OWNERS

### DO:

✅ Follow court rules and policies  
✅ Leave the court in good condition  
✅ Report maintenance issues  
✅ Respect posted hours and capacity limits

### DON'T:

❌ Bring outside food/drinks if prohibited  
❌ Damage equipment or facilities  
❌ Overstay your booking time  
❌ Cause disturbances

---

## MINORS (Under 18)

If you are under 18:
✅ Have parental permission to use Dayo  
✅ Attend games with adult supervision  
✅ Don't share personal contact information  
✅ Report any inappropriate contact to parents and Dayo support

If you're a parent:
✅ Monitor your child's activity  
✅ Ensure they follow safety guidelines  
✅ Report any concerns to **support@dayo.ph**

---

## REPORTING AND ENFORCEMENT

### How to Report Violations:

📱 **In-App**: Tap "⋯" on posts/profiles → "Report"  
📧 **Email**: support@dayo.ph  
📧 **Abuse**: abuse@dayo.ph

We review reports within **48 hours**.

### What Happens:

- **Minor violations**: Warning + content removal
- **Repeated violations**: Temporary suspension (7-30 days)
- **Severe violations**: Permanent ban + report to authorities

---

## ZERO TOLERANCE POLICIES

**Instant permanent ban** for:

- Child endangerment or exploitation
- Threats of physical violence
- Doxxing (sharing private information)
- Large-scale fraud or scamming
- Hacking or security breaches
