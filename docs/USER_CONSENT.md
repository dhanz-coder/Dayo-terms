# USER CONSENT NOTICE

**Effective Date: February 9, 2026**

Welcome to **Dayo**! Before you start using our app, we need your consent to collect and process your personal information.

This notice is part of our commitment to the **Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173)** of the Philippines.

---

## WHAT WE NEED YOUR CONSENT FOR

By clicking "I Accept" or creating an account, you give us permission to:

### 1. **Collect Your Personal Information**

- Name, email, phone number, date of birth
- Profile photo and bio
- Location (city/province and GPS coordinates when you use maps)
- Sports statistics (games played, wins/losses, ratings)

### 2. **Process Your Identity (For Verified Users & Referees)**

- Government-issued ID scanning
- Selfie and face verification
- ID number and address extraction

### 3. **Store Your Content**

- Posts, photos, and videos you upload
- Comments and reactions
- Team rosters and club information
- Court bookings and match results

### 4. **Track Your Activity**

- App usage patterns
- Features you interact with
- Device information (type, OS, IP address)

### 5. **Access Device Permissions**

- **Camera**: To take photos for posts and verification
- **Photo Library**: To upload images/videos
- **Location**: To find nearby courts and show your city
- **Notifications**: To send match invites and updates

### 6. **Share Data with Our Partners**

- **Firebase (Google)**: Authentication and database
- **Cloudflare R2**: Media storage (photos/videos)
- **Persona**: Identity verification
- **Mapbox**: Maps and navigation
- **Google Sign-In**: OAuth login

### 7. **Send You Notifications**

- Match invitations
- Booking confirmations
- Team updates
- App announcements

### 8. **Display Your Profile Publicly**

Your profile name, photo, location (city), and stats will be visible to other users.

---

## HOW YOUR DATA IS PROTECTED

We use:

- **Encryption** (TLS/SSL for data in transit, AES-256 for storage)
- **Secure cloud services** (Firebase, Cloudflare, Persona)
- **Access controls** (only authorized personnel can access data)
- **Regular security audits** to prevent breaches

---

## YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:

✅ **Access** your data (view what we have)  
✅ **Correct** inaccurate information (edit your profile)  
✅ **Delete** your account and data (Profile → Privacy & Security → Delete Account)  
✅ **Withdraw consent** at any time (by deleting your account)  
✅ **Request a copy** of your data (contact privacy@dayo.ph)  
✅ **File a complaint** with the National Privacy Commission

---

## WITHDRAWAL OF CONSENT

You can withdraw your consent at any time by:

1. Deleting your account: **Profile → Privacy & Security → Delete Account**
2. Emailing us: **privacy@dayo.ph**

**Note:** Withdrawing consent means you cannot use the App. Some data may be retained for legal compliance (e.g., verification records, dispute resolution).

- Your parent/guardian consents to the collection and processing of your personal data

## OPTIONAL CONSENTS

Some consents are optional. You can choose to:

### **Do Not Share** (You can still use the App)

- Precise GPS location (you'll only share your city)
- Push notifications (you can disable them in settings)
- Profile photo (you can use a default avatar)

### **Must Accept** (Required to use the App)

- Collection of name, email, phone number
- Storage of posts and content you create
- Display of your profile to other users
- Data processing by Firebase and Cloudflare

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

- Keep you logged in
- Analyze app usage (Firebase Analytics)
- Improve performance and fix bugs

## INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS

Your data may be processed outside the Philippines by:

- **Google (Firebase)** – US and Asia-Pacific data centers
- **Cloudflare (R2)** – Global CDN with Philippine edge servers
- **Persona** – US and EU data centers

These transfers are protected by encryption and contractual agreements.

## DATA RETENTION

We keep your data:

- **While your account is active**
- **30 days after deletion** (for backup recovery)
- **5 years for verification records** (Philippine legal requirement)

Logs and analytics are deleted after **12 months**.
