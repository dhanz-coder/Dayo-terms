## 2. COURT BOOKINGS

### 2.1 How Bookings Work

1. Users browse available courts on the App
2. Users select date, time, and duration
3. Booking request is sent to the court owner
4. Court owner **accepts or declines** the booking
5. **If accepted:** Both parties coordinate directly
6. **Payment is made directly to the court owner** (cash, GCash, Maya, etc.)

### 2.2 Dayo's Responsibilities

Dayo provides the technology to:

- Display court listings
- Send booking requests
- Notify both parties of confirmations or cancellations

Dayo does **NOT**:

- Guarantee court availability
- Inspect or certify court conditions
- Enforce bookings
- Process payments
- Mediate payment disputes

### 2.3 Your Responsibilities

As the person booking:

- Confirm booking details (date, time, rate) with the court owner
- Arrive on time
- Pay the agreed fee directly to the court owner
- Cancel if you cannot attend (as per court cancellation policy)
- Leave the court in good condition

### 2.4 Court Owner Responsibil ities

As a court owner:

- Keep your listing information accurate (rates, hours, address)
- Respond to booking requests promptly
- Honor confirmed bookings
- Maintain safe and playable court conditions
- Communicate cancellations or changes clearly

### 2.5 Cancellations

- **User-initiated cancellations**: May be subject to court cancellation policy (fees may apply)
- **Court owner cancellations**: Court owner should notify users as soon as possible
- **Last-minute cancellations**: May result in account penalties or poor ratings

**Dayo is NOT responsible for**:

- Refunds (handled by court owner)
- Cancellation fees (set by court owner)
- No-shows by either party

## 3. PAYMENTS

### 3.1 NO PAYMENT PROCESSING BY DAYO

**CRITICAL:** Dayo does **NOT**:
❌ Process credit card or debit card payments  
❌ Hold money in escrow  
❌ Act as a payment intermediary  
❌ Handle cash transactions  
❌ Facilitate refunds  
❌ Guarantee payment from any user

### 3.2 Third-Party Payments

**ALL payments are made DIRECTLY between users and service providers using:**

💰 **Cash** (in person)  
💳 **GCash** (peer-to-peer transfer)  
💳 **Maya (PayMaya)** (peer-to-peer transfer)  
💳 **GoTyme** (peer-to-peer transfer)  
💳 **Bank Transfer** (BPI, BDO, etc.)  
💳 **Other mutually agreed methods**

**Dayo does NOT have access to these payment methods and cannot:**

- Cancel payments
- Issue refunds
- Reverse transactions
- Dispute charges
- Recover unpaid fees

### 3.3 Payment Obligations

**BY USING DAYO, YOU AGREE:**

✅ To pay ALL fees directly to the service provider (court owner, referee, league organizer)  
✅ To pay **before or immediately after** the service is provided (as agreed)  
✅ That payment disputes are resolved **directly with the provider**  
✅ That Dayo has **NO financial obligation** if anyone fails to pay

### 3.4 Fee Transparency

- **Court fees:** Set by court owners (hourly rate displayed in listings)
- **Referee fees:** Set by referees (rate per game displayed in profiles)
- **League fees:** Set by league organizers (registration fee shown in league details)

**Users see fees BEFORE booking. No hidden charges by Dayo.**

**Note:** Dayo may introduce a small platform service fee in the future (users will be notified 30 days in advance).

## 4. REFEREE SERVICES

### 4.1 How Referee Requests Work

1. Users request a referee for their match
2. Referees accept or decline based on availability
3. Referee and users agree on fee and match details
4. **Payment is made directly to the referee** (cash, e-wallet, etc.)

### 4.2 Referee Status

- Referees are **independent contractors**, NOT employees of Dayo
- Referees set their own rates, schedule, and terms
- Referees are responsible for their own taxes and insurance

### 4.3 Dayo's Limitations

Dayo does **NOT**:

- Employ or supervise referees
- Guarantee referee availability
- Certify referee qualifications (users should verify)
- Guarantee referee performance or fairness
- Mediate payment disputes with referees

### 4.4 Referee Disputes

If you have a dispute with a referee (no-show, unfair officiating, payment disagreement):

1. Try to resolve it directly with the referee
2. Leave an honest rating and review
3. Report serious violations to **support@dayo.ph**

**Dayo cannot force payment or provide refunds.**

## 6. PAYMENT DISPUTES

- Incomplete or unsatisfactory services

### 6.2 How to Resolve

## 7. FRAUD AND SCAMS

### 7.1 Warning Signs

🚩 Requests for advance payment outside the App  
🚩 Suspiciously low court prices  
🚩 Court owners who disappear after receiving payment  
🚩 Referees who ask for payment before the match without agreement  
🚩 Fake league organizers collecting fees

### 7.2 Protect Yourself

✅ Verify court addresses (check on Google Maps)  
✅ Meet in person or video call before paying large amounts  
✅ Use verified courts and referees (look for verification badges)  
✅ Report suspicious users to **abuse@dayo.ph**  
✅ Never send payment to unverified accounts

### 7.3 Zero Tolerance for Fraud

- Users caught scamming or defrauding others will be **permanently banned**
- Serious cases will be reported to authorities
