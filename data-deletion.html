Data Deletion & Account Removal

Last updated: April 2024 | Effective Date: April 2024

1. Overview

This policy explains how you can request permanent deletion of your YourApp account and associated personal data. We respect your privacy and provide options for complete account removal.

[This section will outline YourApp's commitment to user data deletion rights]

2. What Gets Deleted

Upon account deletion, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Account profile and personal information
  • Contact information (email, phone, address)
  • Payment and billing history
  • Transaction records and purchase history
  • User preferences and settings
  • In-app content created or uploaded by you
  • Friend lists and connections
  • All backup copies of your data
[This section will specify all data categories subject to deletion]

3. What is NOT Deleted

Some information may be retained for legal and operational reasons:

  • Anonymous usage statistics (cannot be linked to you)
  • Communications with support (may be retained for legal compliance)
  • Content generated by others mentioning you (unless specifically requested)
  • Data required for fraud prevention or security (anonymized)
  • Legally required financial records (per tax/regulatory requirements)
[This section will explain data retention exceptions required by law]

4. How to Request Data Deletion

You can request account deletion in three ways:

Method 1: In-App Deletion

  1. Open YourApp and log in to your account
  2. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account
  3. Select "Request Permanent Deletion"
  4. Confirm your decision when prompted
  5. You will receive a confirmation email

Method 2: Email Request

Send an email to privacy@yourapp.com with:

  • Subject: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Your registered email address
  • Your full name
  • Confirmation that you understand deletion is permanent

Method 3: Support Portal

Submit a deletion request through our support portal at support.yourapp.com with verification details.

[This section will provide comprehensive instructions for requesting data deletion]

5. Verification Process

To prevent unauthorized account deletions, we require verification:

  • You must be logged into your account or provide account credentials
  • We will verify ownership via email confirmation link
  • We may request additional identity verification for security
  • Verification typically takes 1-2 business days
[This section will outline security verification procedures]

6. Processing Timeline

Data deletion follows this timeline:

  • Request Submission: Immediate
  • Verification: 1-2 business days
  • Account Deactivation: Immediate upon verification
  • Data Deletion: Up to 30 days from verification
  • Confirmation Email: Sent when deletion is complete
[This section will detail deletion processing timelines]

7. Deactivation vs. Deletion

There are two options:

Account Deactivation (Temporary)

  • Account is hidden from the platform
  • You can reactivate at any time within 90 days
  • All data is preserved
  • Good if you want to take a break

Permanent Deletion

  • Account and all data are permanently removed
  • Cannot be undone or recovered
  • You cannot reuse the same email for a new account for 30 days
  • Final once the process is complete
[This section will clarify the difference between deactivation and deletion]

8. Download Your Data (Before Deletion)

Before requesting deletion, you can download a copy of your data:

  1. Go to Settings → Privacy → Download My Data
  2. Select which data categories you want to download
  3. Request will be prepared within 7 business days
  4. Download link sent via email (valid for 30 days)
[This section will explain the data download/export process]

9. Cancelling a Deletion Request

If you have requested deletion but change your mind:

  • You can cancel within 30 days of the request
  • Log in to your account and go to Settings → Cancel Deletion
  • Or email privacy@yourapp.com to cancel
  • Once data is deleted, cancellation is no longer possible
[This section will explain the cancellation process]

10. Impact of Deletion

Please note that permanent account deletion:

  • Ends all services and benefits immediately
  • Forfeits any virtual currency or credits
  • Removes access to purchased items (unless with persistent license)
  • Ends any active subscriptions
  • Removes all saved data, preferences, and progress
[This section will outline the consequences of permanent deletion]

11. Legal & Compliance

Data deletion requests are processed in compliance with:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
  • Local data protection laws in your jurisdiction
  • YourApp's retention policies
