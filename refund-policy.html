Refund Policy

Last updated: April 2024 | Effective Date: April 2024

1. Overview

YourApp is committed to ensuring customer satisfaction. This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which purchases can be refunded and the process to request a refund.

[This section will provide a high-level overview of YourApp's refund philosophy]

2. Refundable Purchases

The following purchases are eligible for refunds:

  • In-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Premium features and add-ons
  • Virtual currency purchases
  • Digital goods and services
[This section will detail all product categories eligible for refunds]

3. Refund Eligibility

To be eligible for a refund, the following conditions must be met:

  • Request submitted within 30 days of purchase
  • Item not previously refunded
  • Digital goods not fully consumed or used
  • No violation of Terms of Service
  • Valid and original purchase receipt
[This section will specify refund eligibility criteria and time windows]

4. Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Purchased items that have been fully used or consumed
  • Duplicate refunds on the same purchase
  • Purchases obtained fraudulently
  • Items deleted or removed from account
  • Expired or cancelled subscriptions beyond the refund window
[This section will clarify which items and circumstances are explicitly non-refundable]

5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, follow these steps:

  1. Open the YourApp application
  2. Navigate to Settings → Purchase History
  3. Select the purchase you wish to refund
  4. Click "Request Refund" and select a reason
  5. Submit your request

Alternatively, contact support directly at refunds@yourapp.com

[This section will provide step-by-step refund request instructions]

6. Processing Timeline

Refunds are processed as follows:

  • Review: 5-7 business days
  • Approval/Denial: Up to 15 business days
  • Payment Processing: 5-10 business days after approval
  • Total Time: 15-30 business days
[This section will detail refund processing timelines for different payment methods]

7. Refund Methods

Refunds are issued to the original payment method used for the purchase:

  • Credit/Debit Card: Credited back to card
  • Digital Wallet: Refunded to wallet
  • Subscription Credits: Applied as account credit
[This section will explain how refunds are returned based on payment method]

8. Disputes & Appeals

If your refund request is denied, you may appeal the decision within 14 days by providing additional context or documentation to our support team.

9. Contact Refund Support

For refund inquiries or issues:

Email: refunds@yourapp.com
Response Time: 2-3 business days
