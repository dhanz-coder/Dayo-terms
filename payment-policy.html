Payment Policy

Last updated: April 2024 | Effective Date: April 2024

1. Overview

This Payment Policy outlines the terms and conditions for all financial transactions within YourApp, including payment processing, security, and dispute resolution.

[This section will provide an overview of YourApp's payment principles and commitments]

2. Accepted Payment Methods

YourApp accepts the following payment methods:

  • Credit Cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
  • Debit Cards: All major debit card brands
  • Digital Wallets: Apple Pay, Google Pay, PayPal
  • Platform Payment Systems: App Store, Google Play, Samsung Pay
  • Gift Cards: YourApp-issued gift cards and promotional codes
[This section will detail all accepted payment methods and payment processors]

3. Payment Processing

All payments are processed securely through trusted third-party payment processors. Your payment information is encrypted and never stored on YourApp servers.

  • Authorization: Payments are authorized at the time of transaction
  • Processing Time: Transactions typically complete within 5-10 minutes
  • Confirmation: You will receive an email confirmation with receipt
  • Currency: Prices displayed in your local currency based on your location
[This section will explain payment processing procedures and timelines]

4. Pricing & Billing

All prices are clearly displayed before checkout. Prices include applicable taxes based on your location. YourApp reserves the right to adjust prices with 30 days' notice.

[This section will detail pricing policies including taxes and price changes]

5. Subscription Billing

For recurring subscription purchases:

  • You will be billed on the same day each month or period
  • You will receive a reminder email before each billing cycle
  • Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled
  • You can cancel anytime from Settings → Subscriptions
  • Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period
[This section will detail subscription billing terms and cancellation procedures]

6. Payment Security

YourApp implements industry-standard security measures:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all transactions
  • PCI DSS compliance for payment processing
  • Two-factor authentication available for accounts
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • No storage of complete credit card information
[This section will detail payment security standards and certifications]

7. Payment Failures

If a payment fails:

  • You will receive a notification with the reason
  • You may retry the transaction immediately
  • If payment fails multiple times, your subscription may be paused
  • You have 30 days to resolve payment before service is suspended
[This section will explain how payment failures are handled]

8. Disputed Charges

If you dispute a charge:

  1. Contact our support team within 60 days of the charge
  2. Provide transaction details and reason for dispute
  3. We will investigate and respond within 10 business days
  4. If verified as error, funds will be refunded immediately

For credit card disputes, contact your credit card issuer directly.

[This section will outline the dispute resolution process]

9. Late Payment & Suspension

If a payment remains unsuccessful after 30 days:

  • Premium features will be temporarily suspended
  • Your account will receive daily reminder notifications
  • After 60 days, your account may be permanently terminated
  • All unpaid balances remain due
[This section will describe consequences of unpaid charges]

10. Contact Billing Support

For payment issues or inquiries:

Email: billing@yourapp.com
Response Time: 24-48 hours
